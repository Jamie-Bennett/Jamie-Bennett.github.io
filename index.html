Flocking Simulation

Above is a picture of one of the projects I have worked on in my spare time. Namely, my attempt to recreate the flocking simulation "boids". The simulation makes the boids appear to be flocking together. Amazingly this is achieved by making each boid adhere to only a few rules:

Cohesion: Boids will steer towards the center of flockmates in its view.
Seperation: Boids will steer to avoid colliding with neighboors.
Alignment: Boids will fly in the same heading as their flockmates.
